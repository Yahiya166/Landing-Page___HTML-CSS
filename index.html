What is it?

A conference designed to empower you with knowledge and skills to deal with the most pressing problems of running a dental practice. Network in an informal and welcoming space - grow your network so you will never feel alone in your role. For those who are seasoned mangers and those who are new or aspiring managers.

Why is it important?

Being a manager can be a lonely space - you cannot be friends with your team only friendly and the same with your boss. At this conference you can ask the difficult questions and get answers that you can implement immediately - AND you can meet like minded colleagues. After all who is going to invest their time and money if they do not want to grow and be successful?
